Privacy Policy for Fireworks Show Simulator by Simplay Studio

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Fireworks Show Simulator, developed and operated by Simplay Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy and explaining how information is handled when you use the app.

By using Fireworks Show Simulator, you acknowledge and consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from you through the app. Third-party service providers used to operate and improve the app, as well as multiplayer and sharing features, may collect certain data on our behalf.

  • App activity and game progress: interactions, game progress, scores, saved firework shows, session duration, and in-game actions.
  • App info and performance: crash logs and diagnostics used to identify and fix technical issues.
  • Device or other IDs: device name, operating system, browser type and language where applicable, IP address, and mobile device identifiers.
  • Analytics data: anonymized usage data collected through tools such as Unity 3D, including users, session length, and feature usage.
  • Multiplayer data: player IDs, IP addresses, and game interactions needed to support real-time online play.
  • User-generated content: saved or shared firework shows and creations may be stored by us or service providers and may be associated with a player ID or username if shared.
  • Support communications: if you contact us, we may collect your email address and the information included in your message.

The app does not collect sensitive personal information such as your location, contacts, photos, or financial data. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or phone number to use the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app, save progress, support gameplay features, and fix bugs.
  • To analyze usage and improve design, performance, firework customization, and multiplayer experiences.
  • To enable multiplayer features such as real-time play with friends.
  • To store, load, and share firework show creations when you choose to use those features.
  • To respond to support requests and comply with applicable laws or legal requests.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. We may share information in these cases:

  • Third-party service providers: Unity 3D and multiplayer service providers may process app activity, device information, performance data, player IDs, IP addresses, and game interactions.
  • Other players: shared firework show creations may be visible to other players and associated with a player ID or username if applicable.
  • Legal reasons: we may disclose information if required by law, court order, subpoena, or government request.
  • Business transfers: if Simplay Studio is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may transfer to the new entity under this policy.

4. Data Retention and Security

  • Unity 3D analytics data may be retained for up to 14 months.
  • Multiplayer data may be retained for up to 6 months.
  • User-generated content may be retained until deleted or until an account, if applicable, is inactive for 2 years.
  • Support-related communications may be retained for up to 2 years.

After these periods, data is deleted or anonymized. Data is encrypted in transit, and we use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No system is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

We do not provide a separate data deletion mechanism because the app does not collect personally identifiable information directly. Data collected by third parties or shared with other players is managed according to their retention policies or your sharing preferences.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can choose whether to participate in multiplayer modes.
  • You can choose whether to share firework show creations with other players.
  • Unity 3D analytics are used for app operation and improvement and do not collect personally identifiable information from us directly.
  • EU/EEA residents may have GDPR rights including access, correction, erasure, objection, or restriction of processing.
  • California residents may have CCPA rights including the right to know what data is collected and request deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Fireworks Show Simulator is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages, but it is not specifically designed to target children under 13, or 16 in the EU/EEA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If we learn that such information has been collected without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with third-party services such as Unity 3D for analytics and multiplayer service providers for online play. These services may have their own privacy policies. Review Unity's policy at Unity Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post updates on this page and update the Last Updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at contact@simplaystudio.com.
